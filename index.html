PAR Generator for Pro WFM

In order for the JIRA to be approved, it must be accurate and complete. Please fill in the form below to generate content for your PS project defect which will be reviewed by an approver.

  1. Accurately complete all required information in the form, then click Generate.
  2. A new browser will be opened containing your formatted content for JIRA fields.
  3. Copy and paste the text from the new window into the appropriate fields in either the Salesforce Case JIRA form, or in a new JIRA defect.
  4. Create a Salesforce subcase for PAR review and dispatch to the appropriate Segment queue. Use a title of "PAR Review: Issue PS-#####"
  5. Anything marked with a red* is a required field and must be filled out before submission.
